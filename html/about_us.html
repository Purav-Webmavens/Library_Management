About

E-Books.com


A Free Books Reading Website






Launched in 2021, E-Books.com is a popular ebook website with a reputation for innovation, integrity and independence. We provides ebooks to consumers around the world. You can read our ebooks online, or download them to a vast array of devices, using our Ebook website. The company is privately held and run by its founders and is the only independent ebook reader with a global footprint. We have our own publisher licenses and repository of titles. E-Books.com is one of the most popular website in the ebook market, with millions of users. E-Books.com hosts over a million unique ebook titles. In addition to our direct-to-consumer relation, we provide services to book publishers. Digital Comps is a system that enables publishers to send secure ebooks to reviewers and instructors, called “complimentary copies”. E-Books Engine is a system that enables publishers to sell ebooks direct to consumers from their own website.
